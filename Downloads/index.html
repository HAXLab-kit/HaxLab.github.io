Research

Multimodal Interaction

  • Exploring multimodal interaction techniques for collaborative environments combining gaze, speech, and touch
  • Real-time multi-user collaboration in simulated manufacturing environments
  • Tabletop touchpad technique enabling finger-based interaction combined with gaze input to overcome pinch gesture limitations

Immersive Interaction

  • 2D image processing into cubemap format for fully immersive 3D road view VR experiences
  • Gesture-based controls modeled on everyday movements for digitally marginalized users
  • Investigation of virtual grasping methods (controller, pinch, full-hand gesture) across diverse object shapes
  • Analysis of objective metrics (speed, accuracy) and subjective measures (workload, presence)

Collaborative Interaction

  • Interaction techniques enabling intuitive real-time collaboration in multi-user virtual environments
  • Assembly simulation with gesture, gaze, and speech modalities for shared virtual spaces

Embodied XR Interaction

  • Upper and lower body motion estimation in VR using only head movement data
  • Hardware-free approach eliminating need for external sensors or trackers
  • Real-time avatar interaction for multi-user VR environments and training simulations
